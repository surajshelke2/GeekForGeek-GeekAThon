Tennis has been a part of the Summer Olympic Games since the inaugural 1896 Games in Athens. However, it was dropped after the 1924 Games due to disputes between the International Lawn Tennis Federation (ILTF) and the International Olympic Committee (IOC) over how to define amateur players.

B
Wrestling is a combat sport that has been contested at the Olympic Games since the first modern Games in 1896. The sport is divided into two styles: freestyle and Greco-Roman. Freestyle wrestling allows wrestlers to use holds and throws from all parts of the body, while Greco-Roman wrestling only allows wrestlers to use holds and throws from above the waist.

Badminton was first contested at the 1972 Summer Olympics in Munich as a demonstration sport. It was officially included in the Olympic program in 1992, with men's and women's singles and doubles events. The mixed doubles event was added in 1996.

D
Artistic gymnastics is a sport that combines acrobatics, dance, and strength. It is one of the most popular sports at the Summer Olympics, and it has been contested since the first modern Games in 1896.
